Prince Mohamed Aly Palace

General Information

The Prince Mohamed Aly Palace (Manial Palace) in Cairo is a breathtaking architectural masterpiece built between 1901 and 1929. Designed by the prince himself, the palace complex is renowned for its unique fusion of Ottoman, Moorish, Persian, and traditional Egyptian Islamic styles, all set within a rare botanical garden.
Exhibition Theme: The museum consists of unique pavilions housing rare collections of manuscripts, Islamic art, family portraits, historic weaponry, and the magnificent Throne Hall with its famous sunburst ceiling.
