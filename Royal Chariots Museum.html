Royal Chariots Museum

General Information

The Royal Carriages Museum in Boulaq, Cairo, is one of the oldest specialized museums in the world. Established during the reign of Khedive Ismail, it houses a rare collection of royal carriages, golden state chariots, and historical equestrian gear used by the Muhammad Ali Dynasty for official ceremonies and royal processions.
