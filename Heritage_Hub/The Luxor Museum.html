General Information

The Luxor Museum of Ancient Egyptian Art is one of the most beautiful regional museums in Egypt, situated in a prime location on the Nile Corniche in Luxor. The museum is distinguished by its unique display method, which focuses on quality rather than quantity, allowing visitors to appreciate each artifact individually.
Museum History and Contents. Opening: The museum officially opened on December 12, 1975, and recently celebrated its 50th anniversary. Collections: The museum houses rare artifacts spanning the Pharaonic, Coptic, and Islamic periods, most of which were unearthed from the temples and tombs of ancient Thebes.
