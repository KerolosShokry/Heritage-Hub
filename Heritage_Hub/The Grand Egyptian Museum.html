General Information

The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is set to become the world’s premier cultural destination in 2026, welcoming visitors at full operational capacity after its official opening in November 2025.
The Tutankhamun Collection: Displayed in its entirety for the first time (more than 5,000 pieces) in two dedicated halls spanning 7,000 square meters.
The Hanging Obelisk: The world's first hanging obelisk, welcoming visitors at the entrance.
The Grand Staircase: Featuring 87 monumental artifacts that tell the story of ancient Egypt.
The Khufu Boat Museum: Relocated from the Giza Pyramids area to a dedicated building within the museum complex.
